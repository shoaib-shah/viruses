Welcome to the Virus Information Hub

Viruses are microscopic agents that can infect humans, animals, and even plants. They are responsible for numerous diseases, some of which have had widespread impacts on global health. This site aims to provide comprehensive information on viruses, their symptoms, and precautions to protect yourself.

Types of Viruses

Viruses come in various forms, each affecting the body differently. Some of the major types of viruses include:

  • Bacterial Viruses: These viruses infect bacteria.
  • Influenza Viruses: Commonly known as the flu, these viruses can cause severe respiratory illness.
  • Coronaviruses: A group of viruses that caused the COVID-19 pandemic.
