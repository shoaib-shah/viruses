Who We Are

We are a group of Shoaib, Zain, and Shahrukh Ghori. For our E-project, we have come together to explore the world of human viruses. Our project aims to raise awareness about the impact of viruses on human health, how they spread, and the importance of prevention and treatment. With a strong interest in science and health, we have researched various viruses, including their symptoms, transmission methods, and global impact. Through this project, we hope to contribute to a better understanding of viruses and their role in public health, especially in light of recent global events.
We are committed to sharing our knowledge and raising awareness to help prevent the spread of harmful viruses
