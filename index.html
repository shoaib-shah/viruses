Welcome to Human Viruses Information

This website provides educational resources about human viruses, including common viruses, symptoms, prevention methods, and diagnosis.

Virus Information

Learn about the different types of viruses that affect humans, including:

  • Influenza: Common flu virus.
  • HIV: The virus that causes AIDS.
  • COVID-19: A novel coronavirus that led to the global pandemic.
  • Hepatitis: Viruses affecting the liver.
  • Chickenpox: Caused by the varicella-zoster virus.

Prevention

Preventing viral infections is crucial. Here are some tips:

  • Wash hands frequently with soap and water.
  • Avoid close contact with infected individuals.
  • Get vaccinated for preventable viruses like flu and COVID-19.
  • Use hand sanitizers when soap is unavailable.
  • Practice safe sex to prevent the transmission of viruses like HIV.

Symptoms & Diagnosis

Common symptoms of viral infections may include:

  • Fever
  • Coughing and sneezing
  • Fatigue
  • Body aches
  • Rashes (in some infections like chickenpox)

Diagnosis is typically made through blood tests, physical examination, and symptom review by healthcare professionals.
